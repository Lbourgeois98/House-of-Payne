Secret Players Testing

As part of the verification process, you will be required to participate in a secret player testing phase. Here’s how it works:

  • Agent Pays for Testing: Cost is $40.
  • Unknown Players: You will not know who the players are.
  • Cashout Obligation: You must cash out these players as per their requests.
  • Transaction Records: I will request transaction records of players I send.
  • Refund Policy: Your deposit will be refunded if the player successfully cashes out.
  • Passing Criteria: Cashing out does not guarantee you pass. Feedback from players plays a key role in determining your eligibility for the next phase.
  • Number of Players: I will send 3-4 players for testing.
  • Timeline: Testing will take approximately 12-24 hours. Players will not arrive all at once.

This phase is critical to ensuring that you meet the standards of House of Payne Gameroom. Failure to comply with the testing requirements will result in disqualification.
